01 — About

A visual
storyteller.

I'm Santiago Jiménez, a 21-year-old digital artist and animator with a passion for bringing stories to life through motion. Based in Spain, I specialize in 3D and 2D animation, combining technical precision with creative intuition.

My work spans from character animation to layout artistry, with real production experience on a short film currently in development — where I contributed as layout artist using Unreal Engine 5.

I'm a natural collaborator — known for my adaptability, fast learning curve, and the energy I bring to every team I join. Whether working solo or in a studio pipeline, I deliver with consistency and care.
